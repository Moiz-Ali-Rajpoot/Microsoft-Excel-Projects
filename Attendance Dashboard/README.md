# 📅 Employee Attendance Dashboard — Microsoft Excel

> **Company-Wide Daily Attendance Intelligence** — A fully interactive, modern attendance tracking dashboard built entirely in **Microsoft Excel**, covering department-wise, supervisor-wise, and team lead-wise attendance summaries with real-time daily and monthly views.

---

## 🗂️ Overview

This Excel dashboard provides a **company-wide daily attendance snapshot** with department-level and team-level drill-downs — all from a single, filterable screen. The dashboard auto-updates based on the selected **Month** and **Today's Date**, making it a live operational tool for HR and management teams.

The dashboard is structured into **four summary panels**:

- **Attendance Summary – Monthly** — Donut-style percentage breakdown showing Present (86.6%), Absent (8.0%), Late (4.4%), Half-Day (1.0%), and Others (0.1%) for the selected month

- **Attendance Summary – Daily** — Bar chart showing the daily attendance distribution with percentage labels — Present at 90.5%, Leaves 4.1%, Late Arrivals 4.1%, Half-Days 1.2%

- **Department Wise Attendance Summary – Daily** — Row-by-row table covering all departments (HR-ISB, Operations-ISB, Credentialing-ISB, Medical Coding-ISB, Medical Scribe-ISB, MR Management-ISB, Customer Support-ISB, Networks-ISB, Admin-ISB, Sales-ISB, Operations-KHI, Customer Support-KHI, Credentialing-KHI) with columns for Presents, Leaves, Late Arrivals, Half-Days, Others, Total, and % Age

- **Supervisors Attendance Summary – Daily** — Individual supervisor-level breakdown (MO & AMO) showing each supervisor's team attendance with totals — Total MO: 231 (95%), Total AMO: 112 (98%)

- **Team Leads & Backup Team Leads Attendance Summary – Daily** — Granular row-level view of every TL and BUTL with attendance status, total headcount, and percentage — Total TL: 261 (94%), Total BUTL: 109 (95%)

## 💡 Excel Expertise Demonstrated

- 🗓️ **Date-Driven Dynamic Filtering** — Selecting a month or date instantly refreshes all panels, KPI cards, charts, and tables across the entire dashboard
- 🏢 **Multi-Level Attendance Hierarchy** — Tracks attendance simultaneously at Department, Supervisor, Team Lead, and Backup Team Lead levels in one view
- 📊 **Dual Chart Views** — Monthly donut chart and daily bar chart update together to give both trend and snapshot perspectives
- 🎨 **Professional UI Design** — Dark header with gold title text, color-coded department rows, and clean tabular layouts — all built natively in Excel
- ⚡ **Operational Efficiency** — Designed to replace manual daily attendance registers with a single Excel file usable by HR without any BI software
